TERMINOXX BANKING-LIFESTYLE APP

Integrate your business into your customers’ lifestyle with personalized services through our social banking app.

Overview

Terminoxx Banking-Lifestyle App. Terminoxx Banking-Lifestyle App Enables you offer services to your customers through its predictive analysis capability. it also helps you study and gather information about customer Interaction, Attitude, description, behavior and spending patterns.

GEOLOCATION SERVICE

Identify change in customer’s location, prompt customer and offer available financial and Non-financial services such as ATMS, Branches, Hotels, and Car Hires etc. in the customer’s new geographical location.

ATM LOCATOR AND OPERATIONAL STATUS DISPLAY

This feature displays your ATM network to customers upon request. It also informs customers on the operational status of selected ATM such as ATM On/Off, Not Dispensing. It also gives customers the Direction to ATMs

ASSET EXPIRY NOTIFICATION

Automatically send reminder to the customer when the system detects an approaching expiry date of assets such as debit or credit cards. It also suggests the nearest branch where such activities can be done according to the geo-location of the customer.

BANK's ATM SUGGESTION

Identify when a customer performs transactions another Banks ATM and promptly suggest your Bank’s ATM(s) within the customer’s location for future transactions.

ATM CUSTOMER PROFILING

Using historic data gathered from ATM usage of a customer, the app automatically notifies a customer whenever the frequently used ATM by the customer is currently down or out of service. It also suggests other functional ATMs around the location.
