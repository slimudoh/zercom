TANGERINE FOR SMALL BUSINESS

In this globally integrated economy, small to medium size businesses need to take advantage of emerging opportunities.

Overview

Zercom Systems understands the need for small size businesses (with a dispersed staff audience) to train and the cost constraints and logistics nightmare that come with it. We've created a specific solution designed to help growing companies like yours train more productively and profitably to succeed on a smarter planet. Built on a unified global platform and accessible anywhere, Tangerine LMS for small business is a (cloud based) online learning management system designed to help even the smallest businesses to establish an eLearning program.

Allows managers and trainers to administer online training for staff from a central location, schedule courses, evaluate knowledge acquired through assessments, and run bespoke reports for management review. It is flexible, scalable, and available all time, anywhere. With no physical onsite deployment and little to no IT support, small businesses too can have an enterprise learning management system just like large corporations except, this is all in the cloud and your subscription is all that is necessary.

If you are a small to medium size organization with training challenges, choose Tangerine LMS for small business. This LMS is affordable, efficient, optimized, easy to deploy and easy to manage.
