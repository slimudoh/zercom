TERMINOXX MONITORING APPLICATION

Terminoxx ATM monitoring application was developed to provide directions and real-time ATM operational status for the general users and ATM managers.

Overview

It is an ideal solution developed to assist organizations with a network of ATMs monitor the operational status of their terminals across the network right from their mobile device.

  • Monitor all ATMs from the convenience of your mobile phone, Round-the-clock & on-the-go.
  • View all out-of-service, Low cash, In service, not dispensing ATMs on the network.
  • Remotely request for ATM service maintenance.
  • Instant view of all ATMS within current or preferred location.
  • View ATM status right from mobile phone without necessarily visiting the ATM.
  • Save time from visiting multiples non-functional ATMs to make withdrawals or other transactions.
  • Depart from old practice of a dedicated staff always in the office to monitor ATM screens in the office.
Features
ATM Operational Status Display

With this feature, a user is able to determine the operational status of an ATM. You can tell is the ATM is On, Dispensing cash, or “Out-of-Service”. Save your users those needless trips to a non-functional ATM.

ATM Locator

Terminoxx leverages on the GPS technology to suggest nearest ATM using the user’s current location. At a signle click, a user can view all ATMs nearby.

Backend Monitoring & Reporting Module

Terminoxx has an administrative module that offers a comprehensive monitoring and management service for an entire ATM network. This module is designed specifically for the ATM monitoring unit and other stakeholders.

Email & SMS Notification

Reduce your downtime by automatically notifying ATM maintenance unit or custodian via email or sms immediately an ATM is down or requires attention.
